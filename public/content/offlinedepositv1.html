Offline Deposit (Version 1)

This endpoint is used for sending an offline deposit request from the merchant system to the PayWalo server.

With this deposit option, human intervention, payment processors verifies and approves the deposit requests manually. This means that soon as a deposit request is received, payment processors will check with the bank if the deposit has been credited.

Using various approach, they will identify the PayWalo deposit with the matching bank deposit record. Such approach includes but not limited to, UDA, player's name (if sent by the merchant system), player's username (if sent by the merchant system), amount, etc.
