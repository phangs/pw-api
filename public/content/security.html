Security


PayWalo Payment Services implements several security measures to keep the security and confidentiality of your data. Only the eyes of the intended audience shall ever see your records.

Aside from the current HTTP protocol, HTTPS has been enabled to provide a more secured connection between servers. Customers may opt to use either HTTP or HTTPS.

IP whitelisting is also enabled to prevent unauthorixzed connection to the PayWalo Payment Server. This prevents connections other the authorized partners.

Employing this encryption, partner merchants may opt to have this enabled on their account to safely send and receive HTTP/S communication. This ensures that data received to and from our servers are verified.

Note:

Implementation of this encryption is more described in each method.
